Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Futbol IQ Academy different from traditional match analysis?

Traditional analysis focuses on isolated actions and surface-level statistics. Futbol IQ Academy evaluates how players perceive space, process information, and make decisions under real match pressure using intelligence-based metrics.

How do player intelligence metrics improve development?

Intelligence metrics allow us to identify inefficiencies in scanning, decision speed, risk management, and spatial awareness. These insights translate directly into targeted training recommendations that improve match performance.

Is this useful if a player already does private training?

Yes. Many players train frequently without clear direction. Our analysis ensures training is purposeful by aligning technical and tactical work with actual match demands and developmental priorities.

How do I get started?

Start with an individual match breakdown or advanced analysis. From there, we guide players toward structured development pathways based on measurable performance data.
