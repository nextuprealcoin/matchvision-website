Comprehensive Tactical Evaluation for Teams

The Team Match Analysis provides a complete tactical breakdown of your squad’s performance. Team Match Analysis delivers an objective, data-backed evaluation of how your squad actually functions within match conditions. Using structured video tagging and tactical frameworks, we quantify team shape integrity, spacing efficiency, pressing behavior, transition speed, and phase-to-phase execution across all game states.

Rather than subjective observations or isolated clips, our analysis identifies recurring patterns—both positive and limiting—that influence chance creation, defensive stability, and tactical consistency. Coaches receive clear, actionable insight into why systems succeed or break down, allowing for targeted training design, informed game planning, and measurable improvement from match to match.
