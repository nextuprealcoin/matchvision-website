Elite-Level Tactical & Cognitive Team Evaluation

The Team Match Analysis provides a complete tactical breakdown of your squad’s performance. Using structured video tagging and tactical frameworks, we quantify team shape integrity, spacing efficiency, pressing behavior, transition speed, and execution across all phases.

Rather than relying on subjective observations or isolated clips, our analysis identifies recurring patterns—both positive and limiting—that influence chance creation, defensive stability, and tactical consistency. Coaches receive clear, actionable insight into why systems succeed or break down.
