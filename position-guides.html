Tactical Intelligence by Role

Goalkeeper

Organize the back line, anticipate through balls, and initiate build-up play with precision.

Center Back

Control depth, manage spacing, and communicate defensive shape under pressure.

Fullback

Balance wide support with defensive recovery, and time overlapping runs intelligently.

Midfielder

Scan constantly, connect lines, and dictate tempo through smart positioning and passing angles.

Winger

Exploit width, isolate defenders, and make decisive choices in transition and the final third.

Striker

Time runs, manipulate defenders, and finish with composure under pressure.

Common Mistakes by Position

GK: Poor communication or hesitation on crosses
CB: Overcommitting or losing track of depth
FB: Getting caught too high without cover
CM: Ball watching or failing to scan
Winger: Dribbling into traffic or poor timing
Striker: Static movement or predictable finishing
